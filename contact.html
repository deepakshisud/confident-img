FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Here is a list of frequently asked questions that you can refer to
to know us better.

What is included in coaching?

Courses covered in the training are NACOSCE and MCCQE2 exam preparation. Our coaching involves online sessions 3- times per week and each session lasts 2 hours with not more than 4 students per batch. The course is 10 weeks long including a free mock test of 10 exam oriented stations. In addition participants get access to highly useful study materials for the preparation of these Canadian exams.


How long does the membership last?

Members are free to take advice even after the completion coaching sessions until their exam dates.


Who can join the coaching ?

An international medical graduate preparing for NACOSCE and MCCQE2 exams either first time or want to improve his/her scores in NACOSCE is welcome to the coaching.
