Confident IMG

Special coaching program for medical graduates in Canada


KEY FEATURES OF THE PROGRAM

Helping the participants in small groups of no more than FOUR persons in a group enabling the mentor to give special attention to each student.

Special focus is given to personalized areas of improvement during the course especially medical knowledge, communication skills, ethics, and body language .

The main program is delivered through Zoom and other modes of connectivity across the World. One-to-one coaching is also available on demand.

A ten-week coaching program with thrice a week 2 hours of highly interactive sessions which helps the mentor evaluate and monitor the progress of the individual candidates.

NAC OSCE Examination Preperation

The NAC Examination is a one-day exam that assesses your readiness to enter a Canadian residency program. It is a national, standardized examination that tests the knowledge, skills, and attitudes essential for entrance into postgraduate training in Canada. The NAC Examination is an Objective Structured Clinical Examination (OSCE).





About Us

Confident IMG is a special coaching program for international medical graduates who are preparing for NACOSCE, the Canadian medical licensing examination. The main tutors, Dr. Vipan Bhatia an international medical doctor, (LMCC) and Dr Partap Odedra, are highly competent in delivering the medical knowledge required for the Canadian medical exams. The program is successfully delivering its services among participants across Canada for the past three years.

Words from Founders
