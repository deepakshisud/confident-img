Confident IMG

Canadian Medical Exam Preperation

Confident IMG is a special coaching program for international medical graduates who are preparing for NACOSCE, MCCQE1 AND MCCQE2 the Canadian medical licensing exams. The main tutor, Dr. Vipan Bhatia an international medical doctor, is himself LMCC and highly competent in delivering the medical knowledge required for the Canadian medical exams.

"When I started this program with the help of my friends, who encouraged me to bring a coaching platform that is unique and highly effective for IMGs, my goal was to help international doctors in gaining more confidence through knowledge and skills before going into the exams. I am highly encouraged to achieve that goal as many students passed with high scores and achieved success in residency positions or practice-ready assessment programs across Canada by leaning through my services."
